This sample demonstrates using the ESRI.ArcGIS.Client.Toolkit.DataSources OpenStreetMap layer. OpenStreetMap is a collaborative, free and editable map of the world managed by http://www.openstreetmap.org which can be consumed as a map service. Click on the radio buttons to change the various OpenStreetMap Styles to different OpenStreetMapLayer.MapStyle enumerations (like: Mapnik, Osmarender, CycleMap, and NoName).
